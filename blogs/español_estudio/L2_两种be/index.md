# Lesson 2 两种助动词

<br>

![](西语-(to)be.svg)

## Example

> **Yo soy** un programador.

(programador: programmer, a kind of **O**ccupation)

> **Él estar** triste.

(triste: sad, a kind of **T**emporary feeling)
